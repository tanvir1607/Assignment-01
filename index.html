FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about the conference, registration, tickets, speakers, and more.

Who can attend the conference?

Anyone passionate about technology, software development, AI, cloud computing, or open-source is welcome to join.

Will the sessions be recorded?

Yes. All keynote sessions and technical talks will be available to registered attendees after the event.

Do I need prior programming experience?

Not necessarily. We have beginner-friendly workshops as well as advanced sessions for experienced developers.

Will I receive a participation certificate?

Yes. Every registered attendee who checks in at the event will receive a digital participation certificate.

How can I become a speaker?

Submit your proposal through our Call for Speakers page before the submission deadline.
